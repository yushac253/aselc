About ASELC

ASELC is an online based English Language Club. It aims to help individuals in improving & excelling their English skills and making a space to practice & develop fluency.

ASELC comes with a wide varaieties of facilities. Here, the members & their improvement are focused precisely. For this, they are provided with a Group and various tasks to practise english with partners of same interest. While focusing on English, we also help build their other relevent skills.

Title ASELC

The title ASELC have been selected with deep meaning inside it. It combines two different Titles; 'After School' & 'English Language Club'.

It is an English Language Club and you've realized it, let's come to the first title; 'After School'. Every club is generally founded instituition based. As it is an online & independent Organization, we perform our activities after the school periods which helps you do your personal tasks and also doesn't cause study hindrance.

Why ASELC?

ASELC-After School English Language Club aims to improve the member's English skills by practising in a certain environment and develop other skills.

Our activities will surely develop your personality enrich with skills
